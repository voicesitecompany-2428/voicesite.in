## CONTENT RULES (For Developers & Designers)

1. **Never use these words:** robust, seamless, leverage, solution, platform, ecosystem, cutting-edge, state-of-the-art, revolutionary
2. **Always write prices in ₹** — never "INR" or "$"
3. **Keep sentences under 20 words** wherever possible
4. **Every CTA button has a micro-label** below it (e.g., "No credit card needed")
5. **Tamil Nadu references are intentional** — keep them. They signal "this is for you."
6. **The word "AI" appears only where AI is actually doing something specific** — not as a buzzword
7. **14-day free trial** must appear in: Hero, Pricing, Final CTA — minimum
8. **NFC** is always described as *"tap your phone"* — not as "NFC technology"
9. **Zomato/Swiggy references** are intentional comparison anchors — keep them
10. **All numbers (₹ figures, time estimates, percentages)** are backed by research — do not change without verification
