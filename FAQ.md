# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

---

## 🍽️ QR Menu — View Only

**Q1. What is the QR Menu?**
The QR Menu is a digital menu solution that lets your customers instantly view your restaurant's full menu by scanning a QR code with their phone. No app download needed — just scan and browse.

---

**Q2. Can customers place orders through the QR Menu?**
No. The QR Menu is a view-only experience. Customers can browse items, check prices, view photos, and see active offers — but all ordering is done directly with your staff. There is no online ordering, no kitchen integration, and no payment processing involved.

---

**Q3. What can customers see when they scan the QR code?**
Customers can view:
- Menu items with names and descriptions
- Item photos
- Pricing
- Active offers and promotions

---

**Q4. How does the restaurant owner manage the menu?**
Owners get access to a simple dashboard where they can add new items, update existing ones, change prices, upload photos, and delete items. All changes reflect on the customer-facing menu instantly — in real time.

---

**Q5. Is the menu updated live?**
Yes. Any change made by the owner in the dashboard is immediately visible to customers the next time they scan or refresh the menu. There is no delay.

---

**Q6. Does the QR Menu handle billing or transactions?**
No. The QR Menu has zero billing or payment functionality. It is purely a digital product display tool — think of it as a smart, always-updated physical menu, just on a screen.

---

**Q7. Do customers need to create an account or download an app?**
No. Customers simply scan the QR code using their phone's default camera and the menu opens in their browser — no app, no sign-up, no friction.

---

**Q8. Is the QR Menu suitable for all types of restaurants?**
Yes. Whether you run a café, fine dining restaurant, food truck, or cloud kitchen — the QR Menu works for any food business that wants a clean, digital way for customers to view what's on offer.

---

**Q9. What happens if the owner wants to mark an item as unavailable?**
The owner can update or remove items from the dashboard at any time. If a dish runs out, it can be instantly hidden or marked so customers are not confused.

---

**Q10. Is there any limit to how many items the owner can add?**
This depends on the plan you are subscribed to. Please check our pricing page for item limits per plan.

## 💳 Pay & Eat

**Q1. What is Pay & Eat?**
Pay & Eat is a complete self-service dining solution. Customers scan a QR code at their table, browse the menu, place their order directly from their phone, pay online, and receive a digital bill — all without waiting for a staff member.

---

**Q2. How does Pay & Eat work step by step?**
1. Customer sits at the table and scans the QR code.
2. The full menu opens on their phone.
3. They select items and place their order.
4. The order is confirmed and sent to the kitchen.
5. The customer pays online (card, UPI, wallet, etc.).
6. A digital bill/receipt is generated and sent instantly.

---

**Q3. Do customers need to download an app to use Pay & Eat?**
No. Everything works through the browser when they scan the QR code. No app download or account creation is required to place an order or pay.

---

**Q4. What payment methods are supported?**
Pay & Eat supports multiple payment options including credit/debit cards, UPI, and popular digital wallets. Available methods may vary based on your region and payment gateway integration.

---

**Q5. Do customers receive a receipt after paying?**
Yes. As soon as the payment is successful, customers receive a digital bill on their screen which they can save or share.

---

**Q6. Does Pay & Eat send orders to the kitchen automatically?**
Yes. Once a customer confirms and pays, the order is sent directly to the kitchen. There is no need for staff to manually relay the order.

---

**Q7. Can the restaurant still manage their menu through Pay & Eat?**
Yes. Restaurant owners have a dashboard to manage the menu — add, edit, update prices, upload photos, run offers, and remove items — all in real time.

---

**Q8. What if a customer wants to add more items after the initial order?**
Customers can place additional orders from the same table by scanning the QR code again and going through the same ordering and payment flow.

---

**Q9. Is Pay & Eat secure for online payments?**
Yes. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways. Customer payment details are never stored on your restaurant's system.

---

**Q10. How is Pay & Eat different from the QR Menu?**
The QR Menu is view-only — customers can only see the menu and must order through staff. Pay & Eat is a full self-service system where customers can order, pay, and get billed entirely through their phone without any staff involvement.
